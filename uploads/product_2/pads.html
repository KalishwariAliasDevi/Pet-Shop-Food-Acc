The Pets Empire Training Pads are here to make poop training less messy and much easier. Each pad is made of 5 layers to make it super absorbent and retain liquids. This includes a charcoal-tissue layer. A leak-proof layer at the bottom protects your floor and means there's no additional clean-up. Besides being anti-bacterial, these pads are also equipped with 48 hours of odour protection. Once it has been used, all you have to do is fold it up and dispose of it responsibly.

  • With 5 layers to make it super absorbent
